About Me

My name is Javier Martinez. I was born and raised in Tucson, AZ. I like to game, I enjoy sci-fi and fantasy novels and films, like to tinker with things, fix problems, paint, and spend time with family. My priorities are school, work, being a good father, and living life to the fullest. I graduated from the University of Arizona with a degree in Criminal Justice Studies, I worked for Pima county Superior Court as a Juvenile Detention Officer for almost a year. I then was promoted to an adult Probation Officer. I realized that this field was not for me and decided to make a change for the better. I am now studying web development and I have to say it was the best choice I've ever made.
