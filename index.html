About Me

My name is Javier Martinez. I was born and raised in Tucson, AZ. I like to game, I enjoy sci-fi and fantasy novels and films, like to tinker with things, fix problems, paint, and spending time with family. My priorities are school, work, being a good father, and living life to the fullest. I graduated from the University of Arizona with a degree in Criminal Justice Studies, I worked for Pima county Superior Court as a Juvenile Detention Officer for almost a year. I then was promoted to an adult Probation Officer. I realized that this field was not for me and decided to make a change for the better. I am now studying web development and I have to say it was the best choice I've ever made. One of the biggest take aways from finishing the coding bootcamp with the University of Arizona is that there is nothing wrong with asking for help when you do not understand something. I have learned a lot in the bootcamp and though I may not fully understand how to do everything in fullstack web development I know that I will do everything I can to solve the problem and that includes asking for help.
